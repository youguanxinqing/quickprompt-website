Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-19

Quick Prompt is built privacy-first. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your data.

What we store, and where

  • Your prompts, templates, tags, and usage counts are stored locally on your device.
  • If you are signed into iCloud and have Pro, the same data syncs through your own private iCloud account (Apple’s CloudKit). It is never sent to our servers — we don’t operate any.
  • Usage statistics shown in the app are computed on your device and stay there. They are never reported to us.

Data we access

The app reads system preferences it sets itself (shortcut, launch-at-login, purchase state) and, on iPhone/iPad, the list of your active keyboards in order to offer the matching keyboard while filling in a prompt. None of this leaves your device.

Purchases

In-app purchases are handled by Apple’s App Store (StoreKit). We never see your payment details. Your Pro status is synced across your own Apple devices through your private iCloud.

Third parties

Quick Prompt contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
